Vanilla Mug Cake

Description

Easy to make eggless mug cake in just 1 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 4 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • ¼ tsp baking powder
  • 3 tbsp milk
  • 1 tbsp oil(or butter)
  • ¼ tsp vanilla
  • Pinch of salt
  • ½ tbsp sprinkles (optional)

Steps

  1. Mix all the ingredients
  2. Microwave for 1 minute (1000 watt microwave used)
