Chocolate Mug Cake

Description

Easy to make chocolate mug cake with nutella in just 1 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tbsp unsweetened cocoa powder
  • Â¼ tsp baking powder
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 3 tbsp milk
  • 1 tbsp Nutella

Steps

  1. Mix all the ingredients
  2. Microwave for 1 minute (1000 watt microwave used)
